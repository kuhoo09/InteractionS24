the time a google doc
tarnished one of my childhood memories.

move the cursor around to find all 5 parts of the story; read in any order!

Last week,
when randomly scrolling through my old google docs, I found a newsletter made by 'A' for our Google+ forum. When I tried to find the forum, I found out that Google+ wasn't a thing anymore.

In 6th grade, my friends and I were obsessed with Harry Potter.
So, of course, my friend, who we'll call 'A', and I created a Harry Potter-themed Google+ forum.
Our forum had kids from all aorund the world. We had a system for sorting people in Hogwarts houses, for playing Quidditch online, and even held elections for hogwarts profs and prefects.

The Harry Potter forum was fun. But soon, A moved to a different country.
Even though we tried to stay in touch, we grew apart.
And eventually, grew away from Harry Potter and from the forum.

After reading the newsletter I felt sad.
I felt reminiscent of the times before I was on twitter...
before I knew that JKR was a misogynist TERF.
I realized that even though I missed those times with my friend, 'A',
this memory would be forever tarnished.
I could never think of Harry Potter, or even Google+ in the same way again.
i did end up reaching out to 'A', who funnily enough, also goes to Parsons now. I complimented his design skills that i saw in the Harry Potter newsletter for our Google+ forum from 9 years ago and continued to think about the google+ era.
