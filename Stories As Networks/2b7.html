The train crept along gently, amid ash trees. It slowed down and stopped, almost in the middle of a field. No one called the name of a station. There were some children on the station but I didn’t trust them. In a situation such as this one, you couldn’t trust anyone. I decided to wait on the train till I heard someone call the name of my station.

It was hours before the train stopped again, no one called the name again. This time, I chose to get off the train.

Before I realized it, I felt a large, strong man headed to my direction. And he was Captain Richard Madden.
